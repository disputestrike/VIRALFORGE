# Competitor Analysis for ApexAI Landing Page

## 1. Plura.ai
- **Design Style**: Dark mode, modern SaaS, high-contrast typography (Inter/Sans-serif), glow effects, high-quality product UI mockups.
- **Key Sections**: Hero with video/interactive demo, "Legacy vs Plura" comparison, workflow builder visualization, integrations, FAQ.
- **Tone**: Professional, enterprise-grade, focus on reliability and control.

## 2. Heyrosie.com
- **Design Style**: Light mode, friendly, purple/gradient accents, clean layout, focuses on local businesses.
- **Key Sections**: Hero with simple value prop, "Trusted by" testimonials, 3-step process, industry-specific examples, pricing plans.
- **Tone**: Approachable, helpful, "small business" friendly.

## 3. Fin.ai (Intercom)
- **Design Style**: Premium, minimalist, high-end photography/abstract visuals, sophisticated serif + sans-serif pairing.
- **Key Sections**: Large hero with social proof, "Flywheel" process, performance benchmarks, AI engine technical deep-dive, pricing.
- **Tone**: Industry leader, "Spend millions" feel, highly technical yet clear.

## Selected Reference: Plura.ai + Fin.ai Hybrid
- **Base Design**: Plura.ai's dark mode and SaaS aesthetic (matches the "premium" and "spent millions" request).
- **Typography & Sophistication**: Fin.ai's high-end feel (serif headlines for a premium touch).
- **Structure**: Hybrid of Plura's comparison/tech focus and Rosie's industry-specific samples.
